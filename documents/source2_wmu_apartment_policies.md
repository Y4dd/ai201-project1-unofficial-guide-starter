# WMU On-Campus Apartments — Rates, Policies & Cancellation Fees

Source: wmich.edu/housing/apartment-rates and wmich.edu/housing/info/cancelcontract

## Apartment Options Overview

### Arcadia Flats
- Eligibility: Upper-level and graduate students
- Style: Contemporary design with fireplaces, fitness room
- Contract: 10.5 months (longer than standard academic year)
- Non-refundable cleaning fee: **$150 per resident** (due at signing)

### Western View
- Eligibility: Upper-level and graduate students
- Style: Private bedrooms with shared common living spaces
- Bedroom options: 1, 2, 3, or 4 bedrooms
- Contract: 10.5 months
- Non-refundable cleaning fee: **$150 per resident** (due at signing)

### Stadium Drive Apartments
- Eligibility: Upper-level students
- Style: Two-bedroom units, **pet-friendly**
- Contract: Academic year (standard)
- Non-refundable cleaning fee: **$150 per resident** (due at signing)

### Spindler Hall
- Eligibility: Graduate and nontraditional students
- Location: East Campus, near Graduate College
- Style: Single rooms (community or private bath)
- Contract: Academic year (standard)
- Non-refundable cleaning fee: **$90 per resident**

## Important Billing Terms
- Rent is billed **per semester** to student accounts (not monthly)
- Contracts are structured **per bed**, not per unit — you sign for your space, not the whole apartment
- Financial aid may be applied to housing charges through the student account
- Collection agency fees up to 39% plus attorney's fees apply to unpaid balances

## How to Cancel
Students must log into the WMU housing portal and submit a "Contract/Lease Cancellation Request." WMU responds by email.

## Key Exception — Academic Dismissal
If a student is academically dismissed, the contract is cancelled with **no cancellation fee**. Charges apply only for days the space was occupied or keys were issued, whichever is longer.

## Important Trap to Know
"Students withdrawing after classes begin" are charged at full or prorated rates through their effective checkout date — canceling mid-semester does not eliminate the remaining balance.
